# Code of Conduct

> Build. Host. Scale. Your Way — together.

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of the Kleff project, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting and interacting in ways that foster an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

**Behaviors that contribute to a positive environment:**

- Demonstrating empathy and genuine kindness toward other community members
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and lived experiences
- Giving and gracefully receiving constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility, apologizing to those affected by mistakes, and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best for the long-term health of the community and project
- Respecting people's time — do your homework before asking, and be patient while waiting for responses

**Behaviors that are not acceptable:**

- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind — public or private
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Unsolicited sexual attention, advances, or commentary of a sexual nature
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing) without their explicit permission
- Sustained disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or community channels after being asked to stop
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces managed by Kleff — including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, community channels, and any other venue where you are representing the project or community.

It also applies to private communications where the context directly involves Kleff work or representation.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers:

**Email:** [kleffioapp@gmail.com](mailto:kleffioapp@gmail.com)

Please include:

- A clear description of the incident
- Links, screenshots, or timestamps where relevant
- Any other context that would help us investigate

All reports are handled with discretion and confidentiality to the extent permitted by the circumstances. Reporters will not face retaliation for good-faith reports.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will assess community impact and intent when determining consequences. The following guidelines inform how incidents are handled:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.
**Consequence:** A private, written warning with clarity around the expected behavior and an explanation of why the conduct was inappropriate.

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact:** A single incident or a series of actions that violate community standards.
**Consequence:** A formal warning with explicit consequences for continued behavior. The contributor may be temporarily restricted from certain community interactions during this period.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.
**Consequence:** A temporary ban from all forms of community interaction and contribution. Violation of this ban may result in a permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** A demonstrated pattern of harassment, aggression, or community harm — or a single severe incident.
**Consequence:** Permanent removal from all community spaces and contribution activity.
