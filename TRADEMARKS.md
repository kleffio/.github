# Trademark Policy

This document describes the trademark policy for **Kleff** and related marks. It is intended to protect the integrity of the Kleff project while giving the community clear, predictable guidance on what uses are and are not permitted.

---

## Our Marks

The following are trademarks of **Kleff Hosting**:

- The name **"Kleff"**
- The **Kleff logo** and associated visual identity
- The tagline **"Build. Host. Scale. Your Way."**

These marks represent the identity, quality, and trust that the Kleff community has built. Their protection benefits the entire ecosystem.

---

## Permitted Uses

The following uses are permitted **without prior written permission**:

### Nominative and Descriptive References

You may use the Kleff name to accurately describe the software, provided your usage:

- Clearly refers to the Kleff open-source project or software
- Does not imply official endorsement, affiliation, or sponsorship by Kleff Hosting
- Does not misrepresent the origin or nature of the software

**Examples of permitted use:**

- "This project is built on Kleff"
- "Deploying a Kleff instance on Kubernetes"
- "A plugin for Kleff's container service"
- Community blog posts, talks, tutorials, or documentation that reference Kleff

### Community and Educational Use

Community-created content such as blog posts, conference talks, tutorials, and educational materials may reference the Kleff name and project as long as they are clearly not official Kleff communications.

---

## Uses That Require Written Permission

The following uses require explicit written permission from Kleff Hosting before proceeding:

- Distributing a modified version of Kleff under the Kleff name or logo
- Using the Kleff name or logo in the name of a product, service, or organization
- Using the Kleff name or logo in any commercial advertising or promotional material
- Creating merchandise (physical or digital) featuring the Kleff name or logo
- Any use that could reasonably imply an official partnership, affiliation, or endorsement

To request permission, contact: [kleffioapp@gmail.com](mailto:kleffioapp@gmail.com)

---

## Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **not permitted** under any circumstances:

- Offering a hosted version of Kleff as a paid or free service to third parties (see the [Kleff Public License](./LICENSE) for full terms)
- Registering domain names, social media handles, or legal entities that incorporate the Kleff name without permission
- Using the Kleff name or logo in a way that is misleading, defamatory, or that disparages the project or its community
- Implying that your product or service is the "official" Kleff distribution when it is not

---

## Forks and Derivative Works

If you fork Kleff and distribute a modified version, you **must**:

- Remove or replace all uses of the Kleff name and logo from your distribution
- Clearly indicate that your fork is a derivative work and is not affiliated with Kleff Hosting

You are welcome to describe your project as "based on Kleff" or "a fork of Kleff" as a factual statement.

---

## Logo and Visual Identity

The Kleff logo files included in this repository are provided for use in community documentation and references to the official project. They may not be modified, combined with other graphics, or used in contexts that imply official endorsement without prior permission.
